The Apollo Image Archive

In 1961, President John F. Kennedy announced an ambitious goal of sending an American safely to the Moon before the end of the decade.

On those missions the crew operated handheld cameras, capturing the closest and most remarkable images of the moon the world had ever seen.

We saved all of their photography, digitized it, and made it available here, for you.
